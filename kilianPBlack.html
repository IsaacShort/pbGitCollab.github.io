Cybersecurity

Cybersecurity exists in order to protect our data, services and devices, and by extension, ourselves; from unauthorised access or modification. Without the implementation of cybersecurity, your information would be practically freely available to see. Your home address, debit card number, mother's maiden name, any information about yourself could be easily.

Beyond this, in our increasingly technological world, everything we use on a day to day basis many things can be access remotely. It is not unpheasable for someone to take control of your front door lock, for example, and unlock it remotely.

    Cybersecurity Tips
  1. Use a strong password
    • You should consider a passord manager, such as Bitwarden
  2. Don't post your full address or bank details on the internet
  3. Don't download malware
  4. Do download anti-malware
