    Artificial intelligence

    Aritificial intelligence is defined, by the oxford english dictionary, as the theory and development of computer systems able to perform tasks normally requiring human intelligence, such as visual perception, speech recognition, decision-making, and translation between languages.

    An example of a program developed using AI is a alphazero, a chess playing computer program developed by DeepMind. AI has been pivotal for the development of chess, programs such as stockfish are widely considered as capable of beating even the best chess players.

    But AI has wider applications than just games. AI is used to identify traffic patterns to improve navigation routes, translate text between languages and even produce art.
